Best BBQ Restaurant in Da Lat for Foreign Tourists — Local Guide 2026

Looking for the best BBQ restaurant in Da Lat? This local guide covers top spots with stunning views, English menus, and authentic Vietnamese grilling experiences.

Why Da Lat is Vietnam's BBQ Paradise

Da Lat sits at 1,500 meters above sea level in Vietnam's Central Highlands, with year-round temperatures of 18-25°C. Unlike Saigon or Hanoi where grilling outdoors means sweating, Da Lat's cool climate makes outdoor BBQ a magical experience — especially at sunset when the valley turns golden.

Top Pick: Tram Dung Chill — The Triple-View BBQ Experience

Tram Dung Chill is the most unique BBQ restaurant in Da Lat for tourists. Located at 111 Huynh Tan Phat, Ward 11, this outdoor grill offers three breathtaking views in one evening:

5:00 PM: Golden sunset over the valley
5:30-6:30 PM: A vintage French-era train passes right below the restaurant
7:00 PM onwards: Thousands of greenhouse lights create a "sea of stars" effect across the valley

Price: 95,000-300,000 VND/person (approx. $4-12 USD)
Hours: 3:00 PM - 11:00 PM daily
English menu: Available | Zalo booking: Confirmed within 15 minutes

What to Order as a Tourist

Must-try items: US beef BBQ set, honey-grilled chicken, seafood platter (shrimp, squid, scallops), and hot pot combo. Vegetarian options are available on request. Don't miss the local Da Lat wine — a surprisingly good regional specialty!

Other BBQ Options for Tourists

BBQ Garden Da Lat: Pine forest setting, buffet style, 179K VND/person
K-BBQ Da Lat: Korean-style grilling, 199K VND/person
Street BBQ near Lam Vien Square: Budget option from 30-60K VND, authentic street food experience

Practical Tips for Foreign Visitors

Bring a jacket — evenings drop to 15-18°C. Most restaurants accept cash (VND) only, so visit an ATM beforehand. Grab (Vietnam's Uber) works well in Da Lat for transportation. Book your table in advance for weekend evenings.

Conclusion

For the best BBQ experience in Da Lat, arrive at Tram Dung Chill by 5:00 PM to catch all three views. It's one of those rare restaurants where the scenery is as memorable as the food. Book your table here.
